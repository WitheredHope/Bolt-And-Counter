A mostly standard Vanilla Minecraft Server with a few addons from Vanilla Tweaks:

Afk Display - Greys out a player's name that is not moving for 5 minutes

Track Raw Statistics - Adds 100+ scoreboards that track many different statistics

Anti Enderman Greif - Prevents Enderman from picking up blocks

Multiplayer Sleep - Adds the ability for 1 player to sleep to skip the night

Silence Mobs - Adds the ability to silence mobs using a nametag.

Double Shulker Shells - Makes all shulkers drop 2 shells

Dragon Drops Elytra - Makes the ender dragon drop an elytra on death
